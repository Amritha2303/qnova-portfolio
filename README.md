# QNova – Innovation Portfolio Website

QNova is a collaborative student team focused on building innovative technology solutions that address real-world challenges through creativity, research, and computational thinking.

This repository contains the official portfolio website of **Team QNova**, showcasing our achievements, ongoing projects, and individual team portfolios.

---

## What QNova Represents

The name **QNova** reflects the vision and identity of our team.

**Q – Quadra**  
Represents the four members of the team working together with diverse skills and perspectives.

**Nova – New Bright Light**  
Symbolizes innovation, new ideas, and the creation of impactful solutions that bring positive change.

Together, **QNova** represents **four minds igniting new bright ideas to build meaningful technology solutions.**
